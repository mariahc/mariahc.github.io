My son goes to The Peek-a-Boo Daycare and he absolutely loves Miss Katie! Every morning he runs to the front door and begs us to take him to her house. She provides the children with a variety of games, crafts, educational activities, celebrations, animal interactions, and so much more! She even cooks delicious meals and snacks that even the pickiest eaters can't turn away. In addition to all those wonderful things, the kids have access to an outdoor play area that my son loves! We are fortunate to have found someone as spectacular as Miss Katie; I would recommend her to anyone looking for phenomenal daycare.

Katie's indoor and outdoor activities I must say are amazing and my son is growing into a soon to be kindergartener and although we will hate to say good bye in 1 more year, we would love to see more children grow up and become amazing children by enjoying a spot at the Peek A Boo daycare.
