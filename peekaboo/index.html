About us

Hi! My name is Katie. I began my day care journey in 2011 while still pregnant with my daughter. It's been an amazing experience since day one and I've been licensed and operating the peek-a-boo daycare ever since. Our typical day at peek-a-boo is filled with activities and fun. We do loads of arts and crafts. We make projects and games. We spend as much time outside as the weather allows. Outside we play on climbing structures, toy cars, tunnels, slides, bubbles, chalk, sensory table, and we love to do gardening projects where kids take home home-grown beans! We learn about all the bugs and animals we could come across in the backyard. All our days are filled with learning.

What to expect

Please dress your child in clothes that are ready to be played in and get dirty. We don't always play clean. We love paint, chalk, pens, crayons, dirt, bubbles, slime, play-dough and more! Half the fun of sensory play is getting dirty.

Hours of operation:
7am to 5pm Monday through Friday
Lessons start after the last child arrives. We will have either morning music or an educational cartoon playing while kids arrive and get settled, in order to help kids enter into a calm, fun environment.
